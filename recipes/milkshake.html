Milkshake recipe

Milkshake Description

A cool, refreshing chocolate mint milkshake that will have you craving for more. All you need is milk, vanilla ice cream, chocolate syrup, and a few drops of peppermint extract.

Ingredients

  • 4 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • ¼ cup milk
  • ¼ cup chocolate syrup
  • 1 drop peppermint extract
  • Sugar

Steps to make the lasagna

  1. Combine ice cream, milk, chocolate syrup, and peppermint extract in a blender; blend until smooth..
  2. Pour into 2 glasses and serve.
